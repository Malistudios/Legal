1. Controller

The controller for data processing in connection with the app and game "Guard the Egg" is the person named in the legal notice above. Contact for privacy inquiries: 〈KONTAKT-E-MAIL〉.

2. Overview

"Guard the Egg" is an idle / tower-defense game. It is available as a browser game and as an Android/iOS app. We process personal data only to the extent required to operate the game. The game can generally be played without a user account; in that case your progress is stored only locally on your device.

3. Local storage on your device

To store your game progress and settings, the game uses your device's or browser's local storage (localStorage). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us as long as you do not create an account and do not use cloud saving. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(b) GDPR (provision of the game feature you requested).

4. Advertising: Google AdMob

In the mobile app we show voluntary, rewarded video ads ("Rewarded Ads") via Google AdMob (Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland; and Google LLC, USA). Ads are only played when you actively start them.

To deliver ads and prevent fraud, Google processes among other things your device's advertising ID, your IP address, and technical device and usage data. Google may also transfer this data to third countries (e.g. the USA); such transfers are based on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses and the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

Consent: Before ads are shown for the first time in the European Economic Area, we ask for your consent via the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent dialog. Without consent, no personalized ads are delivered. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR (consent), § 25 (1) of the German TDDDG. You can withdraw your consent at any time in the settings.

You can also reset your advertising ID or disable personalized tracking in your device settings. More information: Google Privacy Policy.

5. User account, cloud saving & leaderboard (Supabase)

If you create an account to save your progress across devices (cloud save) or to take part in the leaderboard, we process the data required for this via our backend service Supabase (Supabase Inc.). The data is hosted in the region 〈SUPABASE-REGION, z. B. EU (Frankfurt)〉.

Depending on how you sign in, we process:

  • Email code login (one-time password): your email address (used solely for sign-in / account assignment).
  • Anonymous login: no email address, only an anonymous account identifier.
  • Profile: your self-chosen username.
  • Game progress (progression, upgrades, statistics).
  • Leaderboard values (waves/levels reached per period) — your username and these values are visible to other players on the leaderboard.
  • technical event / abuse logs (e.g. for cheat detection).

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(b) GDPR (contract / account functionality) and Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a fair leaderboard and abuse prevention). Using an account is voluntary.
